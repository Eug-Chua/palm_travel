Our Story

Palm Travel Pte Ltd was established in 1989, during Singapore's growth as a global maritime hub. For over three decades, we've served the travel needs of shipping companies, marine operators, and maritime professionals.

In 2017, we expanded with Palm Marine Travel Pte Ltd to better serve the specialized needs of marine crew travel - managing complex itineraries for seafarers joining and leaving vessels across the world.

Today, we operate as two complementary businesses: Palm Travel handles corporate and executive travel, while Palm Marine Travel focuses on crew rotations and marine operations. Together, we offer complete travel solutions for the maritime industry.
